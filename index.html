Maddu Mess, short for Madras Mess, is an enduring symbol of IIT Bombay's culture. Since its founding in 1978 by the late Shri Kanakaraj, a former cook at the IITB Hostel 9 mess, it has been a cherished part of campus life. Now run by his wife and one of his sons, Maddu Mess has served the community for decades with affordable, hygienic South Indian food. Nestled within the bustling streets near IIT Bombay, it has long been an unassuming cornerstone of the student experience.
